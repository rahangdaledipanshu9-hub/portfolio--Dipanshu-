About Me

Passionate About Innovation & Research

Currently pursuing Chemical Engineering at the University Institute of Chemical Technology, I specialize in turning theoretical concepts into practical, innovative solutions.

My research interests span across reactor design, pyrolysis processes, heterogeneous catalysis, and advanced materials development. I believe in the power of experimentation and continuous learning to drive meaningful technological advancements.

What drives me is the opportunity to work on challenging projects that demand creativity, precision, and deep technical knowledge. Whether it's optimizing chemical processes, developing sustainable materials, or leading collaborative research initiatives, I'm committed to making a tangible impact.

Beyond academics, I actively engage with professional communities including AIChE and IChemE, constantly expanding my expertise and staying at the forefront of industry developments.
